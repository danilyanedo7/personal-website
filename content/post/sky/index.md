All this time, without realizing it, I often add the sky to the poetry I write. The sky is always changing and dynamic, this is the reason why no matter what mood you are in when writing poetry there will always be a representative image of the sky. The sky appears as the most fertile space for imagination.

Every time I visit a new place, I unconsciously look at the sky and take out my camera to capture the sky at that time. My gallery is full of pictures of the sky and I always remember the feeling I had when they were taken. On this occasion, I tried to collect and curate sky photos that I thought were worthy of being displayed on this blog as a gallery. Of the hundreds of sky photos, I can only display 76 from various parts of the earth. For you sky lovers, please enjoy!
